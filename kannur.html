About Kannur District

Kannur is a district in the state of Kerala, India. It is bordered by the state of Karnataka to the north and east, and the Arabian Sea to the west. The district has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its beautiful beaches, backwaters, and historic landmarks.

History of Kannur District

Kannur has a long and fascinating history that dates back to ancient times. The district was once part of the powerful and prosperous Kingdom of Vijayanagara, which ruled over much of southern India in the 14th and 15th centuries. Later, it came under the control of the Kingdom of Mysore, and then the British East India Company. Today, Kannur is a thriving district with a diverse population and a rich cultural heritage.

Tourism in Kannur District

Kannur is a popular tourist destination, known for its natural beauty and cultural landmarks. Some of the top attractions in the district include:

  • Bekal Fort
  • Chandragiri Fort
  • Kappil Beach
  • Valiyaparamba Backwaters
  • Ranipuram Hill Station
